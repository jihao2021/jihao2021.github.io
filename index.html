Introduction

Hao Ji abtained his Ph.D from University of Southern California and his Master's from University of California at Berkeley. his is currently a Data Scientist at USC’s Center for Advanced Research Computing. He serves as a reviewer for multiple top-tier journals and conferences and holding a position on the Editorial Board of the Journal of Mathematics and Computer Science. Hao’s research focuses on multi-agent reinforcement learning, self-organizing systems, deep learing algorithms and large language models in time series analysis.

Research Interests

My research interests include game theory, machine learning and AI, time series classification, reinforcement learning and etc.
