Introduction

Dr. Hao Ji is currently a Data Scientist at USC’s Center for Advanced Research Computing. He abtained his Ph.D from University of Southern California and his Master's from University of California at Berkeley. He serves as a reviewer for multiple top-tier journals and conferences and holding a position on the Editorial Board of the Journal of Mathematics and Computer Science. Hao’s research focuses on multi-agent reinforcement learning, self-organizing systems, deep learing algorithms and large language models in time series analysis and etc. If you are interested in conducting research with Hao Ji, please send an email to haoji@usc.edu

Research Interests

My research interests include game theory, machine learning and AI, time series classification, reinforcement learning and etc.

  • Game Theory in Multi-Agent Marine Ship Combat Scenarios
  • Digital Twin Manufacturing System Modeling using Reinforcement Learning
  • Multivariable Time Series Classification using LLM/CNN
  • Surrogate Model for Parameter Optimization of RL Models
